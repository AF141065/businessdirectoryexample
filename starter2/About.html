About us

Zapoo is a simple business directory that allows the user to submit a listing for SEO purposes. What makes this website different from the other business directories out there is its DIY features. The user is able to stylize their content by selecting a variety of CSS/Javascript features to make their business listing stand out when a customer visits the listing page. Most business directories usually have one layout that they use for all business listings, and Zapoo aims to be creative and unique with their business listing pages.
